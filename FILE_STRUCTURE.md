*   [requirements.txt](file:///d:/PROJECTS/Xeno_Assignment/channel-service/requirements.txt) — Dependency list.

    *   [main.py](file:///d:/PROJECTS/Xeno_Assignment/channel-service/app/main.py) — Webhook listener and simulation initiator.
